Top 5 Amusement Parks in Hyderabad

The City of Nizams, Hyderabad is a place that has always made people proud of its rich history, heritage, diverse culture, unique language, and iconic monuments.

Also recognized as the Capital City of Telangana, Hyderabad combines the old-age traditions with new-age modernity to hypnotize everyone, whether you are visiting the city for a day or planning to stay here for a long time and offers an experience that is incomparable to any city in India.

Home to stately mosques, enchanting museums and noisy bazaars that are lined up with vibrant temples, crystal-clear lakes, upscale restaurants, appealing shopping places, and palatial malls, it is these contrast that makes Hyderabad a worth-visiting place. And the recent boom in the IT and technology world allures many to move to this city permanently or if not, at least, enjoy a trip to Hyderabad as a tourist for once.

The thing that sets the city apart from other holiday destinations in the country is the stunning landscape views and a delectable range of cuisines especially the famous Biryani it offers. Another important aspect is its myriad Amusement Parks.

Hyderabad has numerous amusement parks that are best to visit if your agenda is to beat the heat of harsh days. They are perfect for families, friends, colleagues, and peers to enjoy a full-day hangover of excitement, forgetting about the daily hassle for some time. So, if you are staying somewhere close to Hyderabad or in the city for a business meeting or leisure, then you should check out these amazing entertainment parks that are loved by one and all.

We have curated a comprehensive list of top amusement parks in Hyderabad for you to choose from and head to your favourite park now!

5 Amusement Parks in Hyderabad

1. Wonderla

The loudest, craziest, and the most amazing part of town. This simply is not just any amusement park in Hyderabad, but a world of breath-taking charm. It’s a place to celebrate the smaller moments and share big wonders. Get on one of the 60+ exhilarating rides, or simply laze by the pool under the bright sun. But whatever you do, here, everybody gets to enjoy a wonderful time together by the end of the day. And that's why we are touted as the best Amazement Park in India!

Fill your day with loads of happiness, joy, laughter, and luxury at Wonderla Amusement Park. Widely known for its breathtaking charm, majestic views, and world-class amenities, the Wonderla Amusement Park invites holidaymakers to do something new and different than just casual dinners in Hyderabad.

Combining state-of-the-art equipment with high international safety standards, the amusement park offers mind-boggling opportunities to spend some quality time with your loved ones. There are adrenaline-fuelled rides for experience seekers, water pools for swimmers, and colorful rides with attractive characters for small children.

Other facilities such as enough parking space, lost and found counter, luggage storage, ATM counters, payphones, cloakroom, and separate changing rooms for both males and females are also available for your convenience.

  • Location: Nehru Outer Ring Road, Ravirala, Hyderabad
  • Entry Fee: On weekdays, it charges INR 845 per person for adults, INR 675 per person for children, INR 635 per person for senior citizens between 60 to 69 years of age, and INR 420 per person for senior citizens above 70 years. On weekends, it charges INR 1060 per person for adults, INR 850 per person for children, INR 795 per person for senior citizens between 60 to 69 years of age, and INR 530 per person for senior citizens above 70 years.
  • Timings: 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM on Weekdays, 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM on Weekend
  • Distance: 45 km from Hi-Tec City, 16 km from Airport

2. Wild Water Park

Wild Waters is the Ultimate Theme park destination in Hyderabad spread across 30 acres of tropical landscape and encompassing a large Aquapark, Amusement park and Adventure Park with a record-breaking 60+ wet and dry rides. It is perhaps the biggest of them all in Hyderabad. You are never far away from fun as the park offers the best of water rides, dry thrill rides, adventure rides, and entertainment options for families, friends, schools, colleges, and corporate retreats. Our food courts offer the very best of local and international cuisines that would take your taste buds on a wild ride. Step into Wild Waters and escape into a world filled with fun, adventure and everlasting memories with incredible packages and with a huge choice of games and rides.

With 21 unique wet rides to enjoy, get in the action, grab a tube, feel the rush and get the adrenaline pumping as you slip and slide through the rushing water. Experience the force of the wave generator, or race down the slides with numerous twists and turns or take a break to kick back and relax in the lazy river amidst a tropical jungle. Come and get your thrills, spills and reinvigorating relaxation at the best water park in Hyderabad.

  • Location: Masaniguda Village, Shankarpalli,Ranga Reddy Dist,Hyderabad
  • Entry Fee: In Normal Season, it charges INR 900 per person for adults and INR 680 per person for children, where as in peak season(10th April to 10th June) per adult it charges INR 980 and per children it charges INR 730
  • Timings: Mon-Fri: 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM Sat-Sun/Public Holidays: 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM Water Rides close by 6:00 PM on all days
  • Distance: 25 km from Airport, 43.2 km from Hi-Tec City

3. Jalavihar

Jalavihar is a waterpark situated in Hyderabad, Telangana, inside a territory of about 12.5 sections of land (5.1 ha). Situated close to Sanjeevaiah Park and along the Hussain Sagar Lake, the recreation center was introduced on 20 May 2007.

Sprawling over an area of 12.5 acres of land, Jalavihar Water Park is another biggest amusement and theme park in Hyderabad that introduces you to a next-level of water games and other slides under one roof. It is an idyllic holiday destination for a full-day excursion and can be visited by solo travelers, couples, and families with children.

An entertaining place with an innumerable number of dry rides, the Jalavihar Water Park allows you to dance to the tune of your favorite chartbuster at the Rain Dance Shower. Here you can enjoy a dip in the swimming pool or please your senses at the Wave pool.

For ladies, there is a separate pool and for little guests, there is a dedicated water play area with fun games. Other than that, the fun park in Hyderabad features an amazing amphitheater and a party zone for organizing birthday parties, social gatherings, and corporate lunches.

  • Location: 22/9, Necklace Road, Hussain Sagar, Khairatabad, Hyderabad
  • Entry Fee: INR 400 per person for people above 3 feet, INR 300 per person for people below 3 feet
  • Timings: 11:00 Am to 7:00 PM on all days
  • Distance: 17 km from Hi-Tec City, 34 km from Airport

4. Ocean Park

Located in Gandipet, at a distance of 20 km from Hyderabad, the Ocean Park is one of the best places to have fun on a summer weekend. It is both an amusement park and a water park with two sections dedicated to each of the things. Being one of the first water parks that were opened in Hyderabad, the Ocean Park has a large area and is home to things that can make both kids and adults happy. The park has a lot of water rides to choose from, including kids’ pool, wave pool and water slides. Some of the most popular water rides in Ocean Park include Pendulum, Aqua Snake, Crazy Cruize, Zip Zap Zoom, Multilane Ride, Wave Pool, Harakiri, Tornodo, Aqua-Glide, Aqua Trail and Splash.

For kids, the park boasts of Jumping Frogs, Free Fall, Candor, Pirate Ship, Peacock Train and Video Games and for the ladies, a wave pool with a separate swimming pool is also available. You can pay an extra charge to make the most of special rides.

  • Location: Survey No: 166,167, 168 and 169, Shankarpally Road Near CBIT College, Gandipet, Hyderabad
  • Entry Fee: INR 450 per person for adults, INR 350 per person for children below 4 feet 6 inches
  • Timings: 11:00 AM to 7:30 PM on all days
  • Distance: 17 km from Hi-Tec City, 32 km from Airport

5. Leonia

Counted as the top most place for a full-day exploration of happiness in Hyderabad, the Leonia Resort is a great picnic spot for families with children. Assuring visitors oodles of entertainment, laughter and rejuvenation, this ultimate destination is no less than a heaven for people seeking the facilities of a 7-star hotel under one roof. The resort has an abundance of high-thrill rides and slides that leave you spoilt with choices every time you visit this place. It features water rides for both adults and kids along with swimming pools, a wave pool and rain dance with a DJ.

For an exciting nightlife, you can enjoy live events such as comedy acts, dance thematic performances, acrobatic/fire performances and thematic shows after the sun sets. Besides this, there are 3 vibrant bars where you can elevate your spirit high with an exotic selection of beverages.

Choose your accommodation to enjoy a blissful stay in a warm and welcoming ambience. Suiting to your budget and holiday style, the resort offers 70 luxuriously designed suites, 18 picture-perfect villas and 261 rooms to pamper you with a classic hospitality experience.

You can also visit the spa and wellness centre to soothe your senses with a therapeutic treatment. It also has facilities for indoor and outdoor venues and party lawns for wedding functions, social events and get togethers.

  • Location: Bommaraspet, Shamirpet, Hyderabad
  • Entry Fee: INR 599 per person for splurge in splash, INR 1249 per person for magical mornings, INR 1249 per person for effervescent evenings
  • Timings: 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM on all days
  • Distance 39 km from Hi-Tec City, 41 km from Airport
